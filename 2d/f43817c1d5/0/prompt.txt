# Redesign Randomizer: Wheel/Carousel Style

## Context
The current result screen is too cheesy — emoji-heavy "Winner!" badge, floating particles, shimmer effects, and "There's your answer!" text. The user wants a Price is Right-style wheel/carousel that keeps the theatrical energy but feels more polished.

## Approach

### 1. Replace `SpinnerAnimation.tsx` — Wheel Carousel
Replace the current spinning circle (which just shows one item at a time inside a ring) with a **vertical carousel/wheel** showing multiple items:

- Display a fixed-height viewport showing ~5 visible items in a vertical list
- Center row is highlighted (the "pointer" position) with a distinct background/border
- Items above and below the center are progressively dimmed and slightly scaled down
- On spin: items scroll upward through the viewport with CSS transform/translate, decelerating naturally
- Uses `framer-motion` `animate` with spring physics for the deceleration
- A pointer indicator (arrow or line) marks the center selection zone
- Weighted items can appear larger or with subtle emphasis during the scroll

### 2. Replace `ResultDisplay.tsx` — Clean Result
Strip the cheesiness from the result display:

- Remove: emoji "Winner!" badge, floating particles, shimmer effect, "There's your answer!" text
- Keep: confetti (toned down — single burst, fewer particles)
- The wheel stays visible after selection — the winning item remains highlighted in the center with neighbors still visible but dimmed
- Add a subtle glow/pulse on the selected center item
- Show "Your pick" as a small label above the selected item

### 3. Update `AnimationEngine.tsx` — Unified Flow
- Instead of swapping between SpinnerAnimation and ResultDisplay (`AnimatePresence mode="wait"`), the wheel component handles both states
- The wheel spins and settles — no separate "result" component swap needed
- The confetti fires when the wheel stops
- Button text: "Choose For Me" → "Spin Again" after result

## Files to Modify
- `src/components/randomizer/SpinnerAnimation.tsx` — full rewrite as wheel carousel
- `src/components/randomizer/ResultDisplay.tsx` — simplify dramatically or remove (fold into wheel)
- `src/components/randomizer/AnimationEngine.tsx` — update to use unified wheel component
